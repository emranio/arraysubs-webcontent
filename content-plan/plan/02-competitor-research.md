> ArraySubs combines recurring billing, memberships/access rules, cancellation retention, store credit, checkout/profile tooling, analytics, and operational controls in one native WooCommerce ecosystem.

## Reusable SERP/GEO patterns

- Answer the query before the product pitch; use a visible TL;DR/key-takeaways block.
- Comparisons need “best for,” “not for,” current pricing/version stamps, limitations, total cost, decision tree, and migration implications.
- Tutorials need prerequisites, current screenshots, expected results, failure cases, and a test transaction.
- Troubleshooting needs symptom-to-cause decision trees, exact logs, verification steps, and stop/escalate conditions.
- Build glossary/entity connections across churn, MRR, ARR, LTV, dunning, proration, gateways, and renewals.
- Use original calculators, test results, templates, diagrams, screenshots, and case data—the hardest assets for competitors and AI summaries to commoditize.

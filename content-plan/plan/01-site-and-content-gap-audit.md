## Strengths

- Canonical site configuration, crawlable sitemap, robots allowing all crawlers, breadcrumbs, structured tables, FAQs, direct-answer comparison copy, and a large implementation library.
- Broad product coverage across recurring billing, memberships, retention, store credit, checkout, analytics, and operations.
- Strong vertical proof surface: SaaS/digital, memberships, boxes, courses, publishers, services, B2B, support operations, and loyalty.

## Missing authority assets

- No editorial article library, category hubs, glossary, research library, case-study program, calculators, downloadable templates, RSS feed, author bios, reviewer bios, or editorial policy.
- Almost no external citations or first-party benchmark data.
- No dedicated individual feature routes; 67 feature records live on one features page.
- No reliable real modification dates in the sitemap.
- No named expert authorship and limited organization/entity separation between ArrayHash and ArraySubs.

## Product truth boundaries

- Automatic renewals are Pro through Stripe, PayPal, and Paddle; free supports manual renewal invoices through compatible WooCommerce gateways.
- Checkout Page Builder is for classic checkout; do not claim it customizes Checkout Block.
- Store Credit, Feature Manager, Gateway Health, Member Insight, Multi-Login Prevention, subscription shipping, and automatic failed-payment recovery are Pro.
- Customer-Chosen Subscription Duration, Installment/Split Payments, Early Renew, and Donation/Crowdfunding are coming soon as of the research date.
- ArraySubs does not have native build-your-own-box, gifting, grouped subscriptions, paid trials, team memberships, native LMS integrations, or broad automatic gateway coverage.
- Settings import/export moves ArraySubs configuration; it is not an automated competitor-subscription migration tool.

## Keyword collision rule

When a broad article overlaps a recipe, the article must explain selection criteria, business logic, failure modes, or measurement and then link to the recipe for exact ArraySubs setup. Never paste the recipe steps into the article.

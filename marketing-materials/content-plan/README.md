## Important caveats

- The legacy search-volume and keyword-difficulty figures in `marketing-materials/research/keywords.md` are modeled estimates, not paid-tool or Search Console measurements. Validate them before production forecasting.
- This is a prioritized portfolio, not authorization to mass-publish 200 lightly edited articles. Each piece requires primary-source research, expert review, original examples/assets, and a distinct search intent.
- ArraySubs has 127 existing recipe pages and seven existing branded comparison pages. New articles must support those pages, not clone their intent.
- Product status, prices, trials, competitor claims, regulations, and gateway behavior must be re-verified on every publication/update date.

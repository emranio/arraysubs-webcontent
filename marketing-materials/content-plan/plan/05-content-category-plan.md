## Conversion mapping

- Awareness and interest pieces use **View Pro Pricing** only after the answer, with stronger in-body links to the relevant guide, recipe, feature hub, or use case.
- Consideration pieces use **View Pro Pricing** after a balanced decision framework.
- Decision/purchase pieces use **Choose a Plan** after current pricing, limitations, and migration implications are clear.
- Product-specific setup remains on recipe/manual pages. New articles should never manufacture a second setup flow merely to capture a keyword.

# Editorial roadmap

## Cadence principle

Do not publish 200 pages in a burst. Use the plan as an 18-month small-team portfolio at roughly 10-12 publish-ready pieces per month, or extend it for a smaller team. Every cluster sprint begins with its pillar and 3-5 support pieces, then pauses for indexing and quality review.

## Wave definitions

- **Wave 1 (months 1-4):** P0 editorial pillars and the strongest setup, recovery, membership, retention, comparison, migration, and troubleshooting gaps.
- **Wave 2 (months 5-8):** Finish P0 coverage and launch P1 billing, gateways, analytics, checkout, portal, and SaaS pillars.
- **Wave 3 (months 9-13):** Complete P1 clusters and publish evidence-ready P2 store-credit, physical-commerce, vertical, pricing, and compliance work.
- **Wave 4 (months 14-18):** Complete specialized P2 depth, original benchmarks, glossary, and audits; refresh the earliest comparison and troubleshooting pages.

## Sprint gate

Before any brief moves to draft, confirm: distinct intent; current SERP; one primary-source set; named author/reviewer; original asset plan; internal-link targets; product truth status; and a measurable conversion goal. If those are missing, postpone the article rather than publishing commodity copy.

## Review cadence

- 2 weeks: indexability, rendering, internal links, schema validity.
- 1 month: impressions, query fit, title/snippet behavior, AI referral discovery.
- 3 months: ranking movement, cited pages, assisted conversion, missing subtopics.
- 6 months: update, consolidate, expand, redirect, or retire based on cluster evidence.

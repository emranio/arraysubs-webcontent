## Data We Collect

### Analytics (Google Analytics 4)

- **What**: Page views, session duration, referral source, browser/device type, approximate geographic region (country/city level).
- **How**: Google Analytics script loaded on every page. Uses anonymised IP addresses.
- **Why**: Understand aggregate website usage and improve content.
- **Stored by**: Google LLC (data processed outside the EU — covered by Google's EU SCCs).
- **Retention**: 14 months (default GA4 setting).

### Contact Form Submissions

- **What**: Name, email address, and message text voluntarily submitted via the contact form.
- **Why**: To respond to the enquiry.
- **Retention**: Deleted within 12 months of resolution.

### No Other Data Collection

We do **not** use:
- Advertising pixels or retargeting
- Heatmap or session-recording tools
- Marketing automation platforms
- CRM or email marketing platforms
- Any third-party analytics beyond Google Analytics

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data.

Data is shared only with:
- **Google LLC** — analytics processing (see Google's Privacy Policy)
- **Hosting provider** — server logs (IP addresses) retained for up to 30 days for security purposes

## Your Rights (GDPR / UK GDPR / CCPA)

You have the right to:
- **Access** — request a copy of data we hold about you
- **Rectification** — correct inaccurate data
- **Erasure** — request deletion ("right to be forgotten")
- **Restriction** — limit how we use your data
- **Portability** — receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Object** — object to processing based on legitimate interests
- **Withdraw consent** — at any time, without affecting prior processing

To exercise any right, email **emran@arrayhash.com**. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are in the EU/EEA, you may also lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

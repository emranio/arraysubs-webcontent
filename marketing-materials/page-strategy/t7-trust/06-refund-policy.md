## Refund Terms

### Eligibility

- **Product**: ArraySubs Pro (all license tiers)
- **Window**: 60 calendar days from the date of purchase
- **Amount**: 100% of the purchase price — no partial refunds, no restocking fees
- **Condition**: No conditions required. We do not require a reason.

### What Is Not Refundable

| Item | Reason |
|------|--------|
| Purchases older than 60 days | Outside the guarantee window |
| Renewals already used for the active period and past 60 days from renewal date | Consumed service |
| Chargebacks or disputes filed with your bank before requesting a refund | See note below |

> **Note on chargebacks**: Filing a chargeback before requesting a refund may result in suspension of the associated licence. Please contact emran@arrayhash.com first — we always resolve issues directly and promptly.

## How to Request a Refund

The refund process is **fully automatic**:

1. Navigate to the refund request form at **`arraysubs.com/refund-policy/`** (form embedded on this page)
2. Fill in: order email address, order number, and purchase date
3. Submit the form
4. Refund is processed immediately — no manual review, no approval wait
5. Credit appears on your original payment method within **5–10 business days** depending on your bank or payment provider

You do **not** need to justify your request, uninstall the plugin, or contact support first.

---

## Automatic Processing

Refunds are triggered programmatically upon valid form submission within the 60-day window. No human intervention is required. You will receive an email confirmation once the refund has been issued.

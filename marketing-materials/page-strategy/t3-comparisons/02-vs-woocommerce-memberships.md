### 3. The Two-Plugin Problem
- WCM + WCS = $478/yr for subscriptions + memberships
- ArraySubs includes a free plan, with premium plan details kept on `/pricing/`
- Maintenance overhead of two plugins vs one

### 4. Membership Features Comparison
- Content restriction: Both strong
- Content dripping: Both supported
- Role mapping: Both supported
- Shortcodes: ArraySubs offers `[arraysubs_restrict]` and `[arraysubs_visibility]`
- URL restriction: ArraySubs includes URL pattern blocking (WCM doesn't)

### 5. Who Should Choose What

| Choose | When |
|--------|------|
| **ArraySubs** | You want subscriptions + memberships combined |
| **WooCommerce Memberships** | You only need memberships without recurring billing |

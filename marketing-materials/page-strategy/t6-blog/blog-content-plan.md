## Blog Post Template

Each blog post should include:

1. **SEO meta**: Title tag, H1, meta description, focused keyword
2. **Content structure**: H2/H3 hierarchy with scannable sections
3. **Internal links**: 3-5 links to feature pages, use case pages, or comparison pages
4. **CTA**: At least 2 — one mid-article, one at end
5. **FAQ section**: 3-5 questions targeting featured snippet
6. **GEO angles**: Definitive statements for AI citation
7. **Structured data**: HowTo or FAQ schema where applicable
8. **Visual assets**: Screenshots, diagrams, comparison tables
9. **Word count target**: 1,500-2,500 words for how-to guides; 2,000-3,000 for comparisons

## Content Interlinking Rules

- Every how-to guide links to the relevant feature page
- Every comparison post links to the comparison landing page
- Every post links to at least one use case page
- Every post has at least one CTA to download/pricing
- Posts in the same cluster link to each other

### 2. How It Works (Visual Flow)
```
Customer clicks "Cancel" →
  Step 1: Cancellation reason survey →
  Step 2: Retention offer based on reason →
  Step 3: Customer accepts offer OR confirms cancellation
```
- Animated or static flow diagram

### 3. Cancellation Reason Management
- Create custom cancellation reasons
- Map specific retention offers to each reason
- Collect reason data for churn analytics
- Visual: Reason management screen

### 4. Retention Offer Types
- **Discount Offer**: Offer a percentage or fixed discount to stay
- **Pause Subscription**: Suggest pausing instead of cancelling
- **Downgrade Plan**: Offer a cheaper plan
- **Contact Support**: Route to support for personal resolution
- Visual: Each offer type shown in the customer-facing flow

### 5. Customer Experience
- Non-intrusive: Flows appear only when customer initiates cancellation
- Mobile-responsive flow
- One-click accept or continue to cancel
- Respectful UX — never trap the customer
- Screenshots: Mobile + Desktop customer view

### 6. Churn Data & Insights
- Cancellation reason distribution
- Retention offer acceptance rates
- Most effective offers by reason
- Connection to Analytics dashboard (Pro)

### 7. The Problem It Solves
- Industry stat: 5-7% monthly churn is typical for subscription businesses
- Reducing churn by even 1% can increase lifetime value by 15-20%
- Most WooCommerce subscription plugins: cancel button → gone
- ArraySubs: cancel button → retention opportunity

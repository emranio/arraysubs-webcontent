## Content Blocks

### 1. Hero Section (Pro Badge)
- H1 + subtitle: "Know your numbers — MRR, churn, LTV, all in real-time"
- Screenshot: Analytics dashboard overview
- CTA Buttons: Live Demo + Get Pro Free for 6 Months

### 2. Key Metrics Dashboard
- **MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)**: Real-time tracking
- **ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue)**: Projected yearly revenue
- **Churn Rate**: Percentage of cancelled subscriptions per period
- **Customer LTV**: Average lifetime value per subscriber
- **Active Subscribers**: Total active subscription count
- **Net Revenue Growth**: New MRR minus churned MRR
- Visual: Dashboard with all metrics visible

### 3. Revenue Analytics
- Revenue trends over time (daily/weekly/monthly)
- Revenue by product/plan
- Revenue by payment method
- New vs recurring revenue split
- Visual: Revenue charts and graphs

### 4. Subscriber Analytics
- New subscribers over time
- Churn analysis by reason (linked to retention flow data)
- Cohort analysis — subscriber retention over time
- Plan distribution breakdown
- Visual: Subscriber trend charts

### 5. Retention Insights
- Cancellation reasons distribution
- Retention offer effectiveness (from retention flow)
- Which plans have highest churn
- Trial-to-paid conversion rate
- Visual: Retention analysis dashboard

### 6. Export & Reporting
- Export data to CSV
- Date range filtering
- Compare periods (this month vs last month)
- Visual: Export and filter controls

### 7. FAQ

| Question | Answer Focus |
|----------|-------------|
| Is analytics free? | No — it's a Pro feature |
| What metrics does it track? | MRR, ARR, churn, LTV, subscriber count, revenue trends |
| Can I export data? | Yes — CSV export with date range filters |
| Does it integrate with the retention flow? | Yes — cancellation reason data feeds into analytics |
| Is it real-time? | Near real-time — data updates with each subscription event |

Setting up a subscription system in WooCommerce doesn't have to be complicated. With ArraySubs, you can go from zero to a fully functional subscription store in under 10 minutes. Here's how.

## Step 1: Install ArraySubs

Head to your WordPress dashboard, navigate to **Plugins → Add New**, and search for "ArraySubs." Click **Install** and then **Activate**.

Alternatively, download the plugin from [WordPress.org](https://wordpress.org/plugins/arraysubs/) and upload it manually.

## Step 2: Run the Setup Wizard

After activation, ArraySubs launches a **9-step setup wizard** that configures your entire subscription engine based on your business type:

- **SaaS & Digital Products** — Optimized for software and digital goods
- **Membership Sites** — Configured for content gating and community access
- **Subscription Boxes** — Tuned for physical product recurring deliveries
- **Online Courses** — Set up for drip content and tiered access
- **Service Businesses** — Configured for retainer and recurring service billing
- **Nonprofit** — Optimized for recurring donations
- **Custom** — Full manual configuration

Select your business type and the wizard handles billing rules, checkout behavior, cancellation policy, access control, emails, and advanced features in one pass.

## Step 3: Create Your First Subscription Product

1. Go to **Products → Add New**
2. Set the product type to **Simple Subscription** or **Variable Subscription**
3. Configure your billing cycle (daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly)
4. Optionally add a free trial, sign-up fee, or different renewal price
5. Publish your product

That's it. Your customers can now subscribe directly from your WooCommerce store.

## Step 4: Configure the Customer Portal

ArraySubs automatically adds subscription management to your WooCommerce **My Account** page. Customers can:

- View active subscriptions
- Skip the next renewal
- Pause or resume
- Switch plans with automatic proration
- Cancel (with Retention Flow)
- Pay outstanding invoices

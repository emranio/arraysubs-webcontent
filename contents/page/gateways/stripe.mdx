        <p>Inside the Stripe gateway settings, enable <strong>Save payment information to my account for future purchases</strong> (or equivalent option in your version). This is required for ArraySubs Pro to charge cards on renewal automatically.</p>

  <div className="faq-list">
    <div className="faq-item">
      <h3 className="faq-item__question">Does using Stripe with ArraySubs cost anything extra?</h3>
      <p className="faq-item__answer">Stripe charges its standard transaction fees (typically 2.9% + 30¢ per successful card charge). ArraySubs itself does not add any additional transaction fees. The ArraySubs plugin is free; Pro is a flat annual subscription with no per-transaction markup.</p>
    </div>
    <div className="faq-item">
      <h3 className="faq-item__question">Does ArraySubs support automatic Stripe renewals?</h3>
      <p className="faq-item__answer">Yes — with ArraySubs Pro. The free tier sends renewal invoice emails and the customer pays manually. Pro stores the tokenized Stripe card and automatically charges it on the renewal date without any subscriber action required.</p>
    </div>
    <div className="faq-item">
      <h3 className="faq-item__question">Does it handle 3D Secure and Strong Customer Authentication?</h3>
      <p className="faq-item__answer">Yes. 3D Secure is handled by the WooCommerce Stripe Gateway plugin during initial checkout. ArraySubs creates the subscription after successful authentication. For automatic renewals, Stripe manages the off-session payment exemptions and SCA requirements automatically.</p>
    </div>
    <div className="faq-item">
      <h3 className="faq-item__question">Can customers change their Stripe card?</h3>
      <p className="faq-item__answer">Yes. Subscribers can update their stored payment method from the ArraySubs customer portal inside WooCommerce My Account. The new card is used on the next automatic renewal charge.</p>
    </div>
    <div className="faq-item">
      <h3 className="faq-item__question">What happens if a Stripe renewal charge fails?</h3>
      <p className="faq-item__answer">With ArraySubs Pro, a configurable retry sequence activates. ArraySubs retries the charge on a schedule, sends failed payment recovery emails to the subscriber, and suspends the subscription only after all retries are exhausted.</p>
    </div>
    <div className="faq-item">
      <h3 className="faq-item__question">Which WooCommerce Stripe plugin should I use?</h3>
      <p className="faq-item__answer">ArraySubs is tested and compatible with the official WooCommerce Stripe Gateway (by Automattic / WooCommerce). Install it from the WordPress.org plugin directory for full compatibility.</p>

<Section
  
  label="GRACE PERIODS"
  title="Two-phase grace system gives customers every chance to pay"
  subtitle="Missed a payment? The two-phase grace period keeps the subscription accessible while giving customers maximum opportunity to settle their renewal before cancellation."
>
  <Grid columns={3} gap="md">
    <Card icon="check-circle" title="Phase 1 — Active Grace">
      After a missed payment, the subscription remains <strong>Active</strong> for a configurable number of days (default: 3). The customer retains full access and receives payment reminders.
    </Card>
    <Card icon="pause-circle" title="Phase 2 — On-Hold Grace">
      If payment is still outstanding, the subscription moves to <strong>On-Hold</strong> for a second grace window (default: 7 days). Access is paused but the subscription is not yet cancelled.
    </Card>
    <Card icon="x-circle" title="Auto-cancellation">
      After both grace windows expire with no payment, the subscription is automatically cancelled. At any point during either phase, paying the renewal invoice reactivates immediately.
    </Card>
  </Grid>
</Section>

## What We Collect

### Google Analytics (GA4)

- Page views, session duration, referral source, browser and device type, approximate geographic region (country/city level)
- Loaded via the Google Analytics script on every page
- IP addresses are anonymised
- Data is processed by Google LLC under EU Standard Contractual Clauses
- Retention: 14 months (default GA4 setting)

### Contact Form Submissions

- Name, email address, and message text — only when you voluntarily submit the contact form
- Used solely to respond to your enquiry
- Deleted within 12 months of resolution

### Nothing Else

We do **not** use advertising pixels, retargeting, heatmap or session-recording tools, marketing automation platforms, CRM systems, or any analytics beyond Google Analytics.

| Right | What It Means |
|-------|--------------|
| Access | Request a copy of data we hold about you |

| Erasure | Request deletion of your data |
| Restriction | Limit how we process your data |
| Portability | Receive your data in a machine-readable format |

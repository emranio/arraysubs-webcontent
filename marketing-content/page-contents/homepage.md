### FAQ Items

**Q: What is ArraySubs?**

ArraySubs is a free, open-source WooCommerce plugin that combines subscriptions, memberships, recurring billing, retention flows, store credit, checkout builder, analytics, and audits in a single solution. It replaces the need for multiple separate plugins by providing an all-in-one subscription and membership engine for WordPress.

---

**Q: Is ArraySubs really free?**

Yes. The core plugin is free and has been available on WordPress.org for months — it's not a time-limited trial. You get subscription products (simple and variable), member access control with 10 condition types, recurring billing with a 2-phase grace period, a full customer self-service portal, plan switching with 3 proration methods, skip and pause, the Retention Flow Builder, email notifications, profile builder, a 9-step setup wizard, and more. Pro is an optional add-on for advanced features like store credit, checkout builder, automatic payments, analytics, and audits.

---

**Q: What does ArraySubs Pro add?**

Pro extends the free core with store credit (virtual wallet system), checkout builder (27 field types, drag-and-drop), automatic payments (Stripe, PayPal, Paddle), advanced analytics (MRR, churn, ARPU, 10 KPIs, 40+ reports), audit logs and activity timelines, gateway health dashboard, auto-retry failed payments, auto-downgrade on failure, feature manager (per-plan entitlements), multi-login prevention, subscription shipping, and refund-to-store-credit.

---

**Q: How does ArraySubs compare to WooCommerce Subscriptions?**

WooCommerce Subscriptions only covers subscription billing — no memberships, no Retention Flow, no store credit, no analytics, no audits. Adding WooCommerce Memberships means two separate plugins from the same vendor, with overlapping but incomplete feature sets. ArraySubs includes subscriptions and memberships in a single free plugin, with unique features like the Retention Flow Builder, skip & pause, content dripping, and a 9-step setup wizard that WooCommerce Subscriptions doesn't offer. See the [full comparison](/compare/woocommerce-subscriptions/).

---

**Q: How do I get ArraySubs Pro?**

Sign up for early access and get a full Pro license free for 4 months — no credit card required. We'll send your license key immediately. [Claim your free license](/early-access/).

---

**Q: Does ArraySubs support Stripe, PayPal, and Paddle?**

Yes. The free version supports manual renewals with any WooCommerce payment gateway. ArraySubs Pro adds automatic recurring payments with Stripe (including SCA/3D Secure compliance and card auto-update), PayPal (billing agreements), and Paddle (merchant of record with tax/VAT handling across 200+ countries).

---

**Q: Can I use ArraySubs for memberships without subscriptions?**

Absolutely. You can use the member access control system — content restriction, role mapping, download gating, URL restriction, and member discounts — independently of subscription billing. Create fixed-period membership products or manually assign access rules. Subscription billing is optional.

---

**Q: Does ArraySubs require the classic WooCommerce checkout?**

Yes. ArraySubs uses the classic (shortcode-based) WooCommerce checkout. This is required for the Checkout Builder's drag-and-drop field editor, conditional logic, and multi-step layouts to work. HPOS (High-Performance Order Storage) is fully supported.

---

**Q: What happens if a customer's payment fails?**

ArraySubs uses a 2-phase grace period system. After a missed payment, the subscription stays active for a configurable grace period (default: 3 days), then moves to on-hold (default: 7 days), and only then cancels. With Pro, automatic retry and auto-downgrade to a cheaper plan are available. Customers can pay their outstanding invoice at any point during the grace period to restore full access.

---

**Q: Can customers manage their own subscriptions?**

Yes. The customer portal inside WooCommerce My Account lets subscribers view details, skip the next renewal, pause their subscription, change plans (upgrade, downgrade, or crossgrade), cancel with undo support, pay outstanding invoices, and — with Pro — update payment methods and view store credit balances.

---

**Q: Is there a setup wizard?**

Yes. A 9-step guided wizard asks about your business type (SaaS, subscription box, membership, content site, services, nonprofit, or custom) and automatically configures billing, checkout, Retention Flow, access control, emails, and additional features. Most stores are fully configured in under 10 minutes. You can also export your configuration as JSON and import it to other sites.

## Executive Summary

ArraySubs occupies a unique all-in-one position in the WooCommerce subscription + membership market, competing against an established $478/yr official Woo stack and several freemium alternatives. The keyword landscape reveals three tiers of opportunity:

1. **Quick wins** — Long-tail feature-specific keywords and competitor "alternative" queries where no all-in-one competitor currently dominates SERP content (e.g., "woocommerce subscription retention flow," "woocommerce store credit plugin," "woocommerce checkout builder for subscriptions")
2. **Growth keywords** — Mid-volume commercial terms where ArraySubs' all-in-one positioning and generous free tier create differentiation (e.g., "woocommerce subscription plugin free," "woocommerce subscription and membership plugin")
3. **Long-term authority plays** — High-volume head terms where the WooCommerce official brand and WP Swings currently dominate (e.g., "woocommerce subscriptions," "woocommerce recurring payments")

The strategy below clusters 200+ keywords into **12 topic clusters**, each split into **landing pages** (commercial/transactional) and **blog articles** (informational/GEO-optimized).

## Methodology Notes

- Search volume estimates are based on WooCommerce ecosystem analysis (6M+ active installs), competitor install/download data, WordPress.org search trends, and cross-referenced against known keyword volumes in the WordPress plugin market
- Keyword difficulty (KD) is estimated on a 1-100 scale using competitor domain authority, current SERP composition, and content depth of ranking pages
- Opportunity scores factor in: `(Volume × Intent Value) / Difficulty` where Intent Value is Informational=1, Commercial=2, Transactional=3
- All estimates should be validated with live SEO tool data before finalizing content production priorities
- Cluster volumes represent the aggregate estimated volume of all keywords within the cluster
